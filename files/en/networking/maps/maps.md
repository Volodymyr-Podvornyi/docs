Maps
=============

This section of the networking module displays a physical location Map with all devices, services, customers, leads. All located objects must have the configured GPS coordinates in Splynx.

![Map](map.png)

On the right sidebar of the map, is provided a convenient filter for you to display objects by a particular criteria. You can filter objects to display on the map by a variety of parameters/types:

![Filter](filter.png)

![Filter](filter2.png)

Once you've selected the objects you would like to see for a specific or all partners and locations, simply click on *Apply* button and you will see the result of the criteria on the map. You can then identify each of the objects/items on the map with the provided legend:

![Legend](legend.png)
